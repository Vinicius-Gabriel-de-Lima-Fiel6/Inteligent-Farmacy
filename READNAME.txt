This WEB application is an advanced platform designed to optimize routines in pharmaceutical laboratories and chemical research environments. The system integrates four fundamental pillars:
 * Data Visualization & Graphing: Capability to plot complex solubility curves with mathematical processing (Cubic Spline Interpolation) for the precise representation of experimental data.
 * Global Database Research: Integration for querying substances, drugs, and chemical properties in international databases, facilitating the search for drug interactions and pharmacokinetics.
 * Stoichiometric & Chemical Calculations: Automated calculation center covering everything from basic stoichiometry to correction/equivalence factors and clinical calculations (e.g., Creatinine Clearance).
 * Data Export & Management: Functionality for exporting results and tables in CSV formats and high-resolution images (PNG/SVG) for documentation and reporting.
